Software from Eastern Kentucky

Explore our available chess software and other projects in development.

DoBackChess and our Reckless web project are available now; our other listed projects are coming soon.

DoBackChess.com

Our chess training web application helps players learn from their own games with analysis, reports, puzzles, and spaced review. Visit DoBackChess to try it.

Reckless for the web

Our WebAssembly version brings the open-source Reckless chess engine to modern browsers, where analysis runs locally on the user's device. Explore the free demo and reusable package on GitHub, or visit the original Rust engine project.

Online software services

APIs
